# SDEV248_GroupProject-
2-D 8-bit game.
Genre: Action Adventure. Fantasy.
Game concept
The core premise is fighting monsters, completing tasks if necessary, and continuing on to the next level.
The player's role is the role of the hero.
What makes the game stand out from others in the market is the maze-like level design of some levels, sassy game over screen, and the overall story of the game.

key features 
Intro Cutscene explaining at least part of the storyline.
NPCs with dialogue options relevant to the plot/story.
Enemies/Mobs that go after and attack the Player Character.
Being able to attack the Enemy Mobs.
Player and enemy multi directional movement that Implement Newton’s 3 laws of motion and vectors.
“Game over” or “Get good loser” for Failure Cutscenes. And "Congrats" or “You won” for Success Cutscenes.
Maze like levels.
Ability to go to the next level in the game via completing whatever task needs to be done and/or moving to a certain area of the map
2 levels.
The player can block attacks with the shield or attack with their sword.
Graphical and audio design to match the Fantasy genre of the game.
background soundtrack, sound effects, and positional audio if needed.
HUD that shows the players and enemies health and anything needed to show progress.
Sound effects
Fantasy background music
Attack sound effects for both enemy and player
Hurt sound effects for both enemy and player
Walking sound effects


level designs
Fantasy level world designs
Maybe have some levels be maze-like for example a town level where the buildings are arranged to be maze-like.
Regular level where you have to complete a task and/or a rest level.
Enemies on 1 or both levels
NPCs on one or both levels

animation(s) to be used
Left,right,up,and down walk animations for both player and enemies/MOBS.
Idle animations for both player and enemies/MOBS or stop animation when not moving.
Attack animations for both player and enemies/MOBS.
Blocking animation

storyline
The Player is a brave hero tasked with protecting his city. The Monsters led by the Monster Queen invade the city and start to run rampant. So the hero has to fight monsters to protect his home.
